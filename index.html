Christmas Advent

Just days, hours, minutes, and seconds until Christmas!

Welcome to my advent calendar. Come here every day to count down the days until Christmas! Starting December 1st you can press a new button each day, through December 25th to get a little something. This something can be a Christmas fact, Bible verse, or a small game (no points though...). You can still access each button through the 31st, but starting January 1st all buttons will be disabled and you'll have to wait until next December to access them again.

By the way it's cold in here, why don't you start the fire? Just give that fireplace a tap or click in the black area. If you want it to be cold for some reason... don't start the fire, it's fine.
